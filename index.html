Overview

We are organizing a workshop on cosmological measurements with neutral hydrogen. The workshop will take place in Berkeley over the three days of January 11-13, 2017. Topics will include post-reionization probes such as Lyman-alpha forest and 21cm intensity mapping. A program and list of participants will be available soon.

Registration is limited to 50 participants, if you are planning to attend, please register here.
